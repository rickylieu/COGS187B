Mission Statement

Kaibigang Pilipin@ (KP) is a student-run Pilipin@-American organization at the University of California, San Diego. We seek to promote a conscious dialogue by engaging in social, political, academic, and cultural topics in the Pilipin@ and Pilipin@-American community on campus.

Pillars

Social

We strive to provide a safe and welcoming environment where our members have the opportunity to create lasting relations.

Political

We aim to be aware of the political environments and give dialogue to the issues that Filipinx face.

Academic

We stress the importance of working towards a higher education and encourage a learning environment.

Cultural

We believe it is our duty to maintain, preserve, and promote the Filipino culture through the observance and practice of Filipino traditions.

Empower

We work to empower each other through support and understanding, thus building each other into better individuals.
