Kaibigang Pilipin@ (KP) is run by students for students at the University of California, San Diego. We strive to provide a familiar and safe space for students to find a community in.

KP's Pillars

Social

We strive to provide a safe and welcoming environment where our members have the opportunity to create lasting relations.

Political

We aim to be aware of the political environments and give dialogue to the issues that Filipinx face.

Academic

We stress the importance of working towards a higher education and encourage a learning environment.

Cultural

We believe it is our duty to maintain, preserve, and promote the Filipino culture through the observance and practice of Filipino traditions.

Empower

We work to empower each other through support and understanding, thus building each other into better individuals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I join Kaibigang Pilipin@?

You don't have to do anything to actually join, just come to events and join our community! We have biweekly General Body Meetings where we can tell you more about Filipin@ culture, history, and upcoming events.

Q: Where and when are the General Body Meetings?

Our General Body Meetings are held every odd week of the academic quarter on Wednesdays, 6 - 8 p.m. at the Communidad Large of the Cross-Cultural Center on the 2nd floor of Price Center East (above Burger King)

Q: Do I have to be Filipin@ to join KP?

Nope, we are all inclusive of all communities and identities! Join to learn more about Filipin@ culture, our mentorship programs, outreach events, and much more!
