Mission Statement

Kaibigang Pilipin@ (KP) is a student-run Pilipin@-American organization at the University of California, San Diego. We seek to promote a conscious dialogue by engaging in social, political, academic, and cultural topics in the Pilipin@ and Pilipin@-American community on campus.

Pillars

  • Social
  • Political
  • Academic
  • Cultural
  • Empowerment
