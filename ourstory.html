Ourstory

Ourstory

-

  • 1987

    Kaibigang Pilipino (now Kaibigang Pilipin@) is registered as a student organization.

  • 1992

    First High School Conference, Pilipino Cultural Night (now Pilipin@ Cultural Celebration), and Pilipino Graduation.

  • 1995

    UCSD's Cross-Cultural Center becomes an affiliated organization.

  • 1998

    The Kuya/Ate/Ading component is created & the first Pilipino History and Arts Celebration (now Magkasama) is held.

  • 1999

    First Pilipino Awareness Day (now Pilipin@ Awareness Week) is held.

  • 2002

    Filipino Collegiate Collaborative is established in an effort to save Tagalog classes from being cut.

  • 2005

    Student Teaching Academic Retention (STAR) is institutionalized as the organization's retention project after the creation of the Peer Counseling component.

  • 2006

    The first KP STAR Retreat is held.

  • 2007

    CORE becomes the organization's outreach project after the launch of the SPACES. KP helps in establishing Kapwa Tao. The 20th Anniversary Ourstory Gala is held.

  • 2010

    KP CORE gains access to work with Samuel F.B. Morse High School. The first Kapwa Tao Kumustahan Oryenstasyon is held.

  • 2011

    The first Filipino Collegiate Collaborative College to Career Conference is held.

  • 2012

    KP changes its name to Kaibigang Pilipin@ to recognize Pinays in the space. The 25th Anniversary Ourstory Gala is held.
