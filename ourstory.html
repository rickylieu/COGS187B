Ourstory

Timeline

  • 1987

    Kaibigang Pilipino (now known as Kaibigang Pilipin@) is registered as a student organization.

  • 1992

    First High School Conference, Pilipino Cultural Night (now known as Pilipin@ Cultural Celebration), and Pilipino Graduation, are held.

  • 1995

    Kaibigang Pilipino gets involved in the creation of UCSD's Cross-Cultural Center, becoming an affiliated organization.

  • 1998

    The Kuya/Ate/Ading component is conceived and the first Pilipino History and Arts Celebration (now known as Magkasama) is held.

  • 1999

    First Pilipino Awareness Day (now known as Pilipin@ Awareness Week) is held.

  • 2002

    Filipino Collegiate Collaborative is established between the Pilipin@-American student organizations of UCSD, SDSU, USD, CSUSM, Miramar, and Southwestern in an effort to save Tagalog classes from being cut.

  • 2005

    Kaibigang Pilipino's Student Teaching Academic Retention (KP STAR) is institutionalized as the organization's retention project after the creation of the Peer Counseling component.

  • 2006

    The first KP STAR Retreat is held.

  • 2007

    Kaibigang Pilipino's Community Outreach to Realize Empowerment (KP CORE) is institutionalized as the organization's outreach project after the launch of the Student Promoted Access Center for Education and Service (SPACES) from the passing of the campus-wide "Promoting Understanding and Learning through Service and Education (PULSE) Referendum. KP also joins Kamalayan Kollective and KaBayanihan (and now Pilipino Undergraduate Society for Health) in establishing Kapwa Tao. The 20th Anniversary Ourstory Gala is held.

  • 2010

    A malicious fraternity party, the Compton Cookout, targets underprivileged, underrepresented, and underserved communities in UCSD, eliciting campus-wide protests and campus diversity policy reform. KP CORE gains access to work with Samuel F.B. Morse High School students and administration. The first Kapwa Tao Kumustahan Oryenstasyon is held.

  • 2011

    The first Filipino Collegiate Collaborative College to Career Conference is held.

  • 2012

    Kaibigang Pilipino changes its name to Kaibigang Pilipin@ to recognize Pinays in the space. The 25th Anniversary Ourstory Gala is held.
